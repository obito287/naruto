Founded on October 12, 1997 DAV Institute of Management is one of the leading Business Schools of India and a part of the largest educational trust of the country.Right from the day of its existence DAVIM is working persistently to impart quality management education, research, consultancy and training.


DAVIM is destined to empower souls across the world to effortlessly optimize potential by imbibing the best of values and beliefs of east and west rooted in the philosophy of Dayanand Anglo Vedic culture.

Situated in the heart of the Industrial town Faridabad, DAVIM provides exceptional infrastructure and a congenial learning environment to equip students with the best of the management practices and to give them a striking edge to face the highly competitive socio economic arena.

Our rigors and updated course curriculum helps students to acquire knowledge, skills and attitude to fulfill the ever- changing requirements of the corporate world

At DAVIM, we don't believe merely in creating a corporate warrior out of a student to toy with the people and resources. But we train students to co-exist and live in total harmony with the world.
