Master of Computer Applications(MCA)


Each student comes to DAVIM with unique professional and personal reasons for pursuing a Master Degree in Computer Applications.One has three years to explore,to be bold,creative and learn as much as they can.The curriculum includes a range of courses that take advantages of new teaching methods and the reinforcement of basic skills and concepts.


The Three years Regular MCA programme is the flagship course of DAVIM.The program follows the structure and curriculum designed by the institute which includes practical exposure to the industry/corporate along with the syllabi devised by the university.

The first year comprises of core subjects concentrating on the basics concepts relating to analytical techniques,basic system administration and programming which provides an orientation towards IT.

In second year the student is exposed to the area of advanced computer applications like artifical intelligence,neural network,visual programming, and basics of networking and web technologies.

The final and crucial year the students are exposed to advanced project management and also undergo a rigorous six month industrial intership.

At the end of the course the student discover their true potential transformed into professionalism excellence.Students gain conceptual,technical and interpersonal skills while fine-tuning on the social purpose in technical world.
