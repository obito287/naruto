Bachelor of Computer Applications(BCA)


Each student comes to DAVIM with unique professional and personal reasons for pursuing a Bachelor Degree in Computer Applications.One has three years to explore,to be bold,creative and learn as much as they can.The curriculum includes a range of courses that take advantages of new teaching methods and the reinforcement of basic skills and concepts.


The Three years Regular BCA course comprising of six semester in Computer Science.The program follows the structure and curriculum designed by the institute which includes practical exposure to the industry/corporate along with the syllabi devised by the university.

The first year comprises of core subjects concentrating on the basics concepts relating to analytical techniques,basic system administration and programming which provides an orientation towards IT.

In second & third year the student is exposed to the area of computer applications like Java Programming ,Visual Basic,Multimedia RDBMS,Management Information System ,basics of networking and internet technologies.

After successful completion of this course,the student can get the job of Programmer,System Analyst and Web Designer etc.Students can also pursue MCA/MBA/PG Diplomas after the successful completion.
