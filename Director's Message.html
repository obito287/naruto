Director's Message


The DAVIM is for those who shall be pioneers, for those who shall experiment with the cutting edge of contemporary thought and creative expression, for those who shall dare give shape to their aspiration s and cherished dreams. At DAVIM, we believe that each student is special and has the potential to excel in many different ways. It is our endeavor to enhance his/her potentialities to greater heights. While the institute provides a blend of academic, cultural and intellectual resources, it also brings to the students a dynamic. environment for the overall development of their personalities, aspirations and talents.


The college has an excellent infrastructure that includes computer laboratories, free internet, a library with computerized catalogue and issue systems, a seminar hall furnished with state of the art projection systems and classrooms fitted with audio-visual gadgets. The Learning Management System (LMS) is an electronic interaction platform for the students and their parents to be in constant touch with the institute and the faculty and can be accessed from anywhere through internet. Extremely courteous and helpful administrative staff are always available to guide the students through all procedural formalities.


The DAVIM experience is a challenging and inspiring one that helps the student discover innate capabilities, set life-long goals and evoke a student's own commitment to utilize her/his potential in reaping maximum benefits from the institute.

Good luck

Dr. Neelam Gulati
